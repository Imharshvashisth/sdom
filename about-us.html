Our Legacy

The Story of
Shri Shyam Oil

1995

Established

A Heritage of
Purity & Taste

The journey of Shri Shyam Oil began decades ago with a simple, yet profound vision: to bring the unadulterated goodness of nature directly to the Indian kitchen. We started as a small, passionate team dedicated to preserving the traditional art of oil extraction.

We believe that real health is deeply rooted in our traditions. That's why we never compromise on the Kachi Ghani process.

Over the years, we have grown from a local mill to a trusted household brand. Despite our growth and the adoption of modern, hygienic packaging technology, our core extraction process remains completely traditional. We carefully select the finest mustard seeds directly from Indian farmers, ensuring that every drop of oil retains its natural pungency, aroma, and rich nutrients.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred edible oil brand in every household. We envision a society where every family enjoys meals that are not only irresistibly delicious but also deeply nourishing, contributing to a healthier and happier lifestyle.

Our Mission

To consistently deliver 100% pure, natural, and chemical-free Kachi Ghani mustard oil. We are committed to supporting our local farmers, maintaining uncompromising quality standards, and employing sustainable practices in every step of our production.
